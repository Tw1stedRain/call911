A Brief History of Telephones and Telephone Calls

The Home Phone

Always on. Always in the same place. Pick up the handset. Dial.

When the phone company connects a call made from your home phone to 911 they instantly provide the 911 center with your complete address.

This is how the 911 system was designed. The carrier is obligated to supply your location directly from the phone installation records. In most areas an emergency response will be sent to that address, even if you are unable to speak.

Always on. Always in the same place. Pick up the handset. Dial. Talk.
